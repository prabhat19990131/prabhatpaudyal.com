About Me

Prabhat Paudyal

Welcome to my creative universe. I'm a "polymath" creator based in Nepal, driven by a passion for storytelling in all its forms. My work isn't confined to a single medium; I express ideas through writing, visual effects, video analysis, and even physical crafting.

This website is a central hub for all my projects. Here's a quick look at what I do:

  • Writer: I craft novels and poetry in both Nepali and English, exploring themes of mystery, culture, and the human condition.
  • Video Essayist: I create video content that dives deep into literature and the hidden meanings behind popular songs.
  • VFX & Animation Artist: I work in the film and advertising industry, building digital worlds, creating stunning effects, and bringing characters to life.
  • Cosplayer & Maker: I build intricate costumes and props from scratch, and I sell DIY kits and patterns for fellow creators.
  • Science Enthusiast: As "The Lazy Scientist," I break down complex scientific ideas into fun, easy-to-understand explainers.

My goal is to build worlds—whether with words, pixels, or my own hands. I'm glad you're here to explore them with me.
